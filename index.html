Vectors

Before coming to the definition, let's see three distinct but related ideas about vector. Let's start with Physics and then understand in Mathematics and Computers Science

In Physics: Vector's are arrows pointing in space. What defines a given vector is, it's length and the direction it points to. Vector that live in flat plane are 2D and sitting in broader space that you and I live are 3D vectors.

In Mathematics: Vector's can be anything where there's a sensible notion of adding two vectors and multiplying a vector by a number

In Machine Learning: Vectors are effective way to store objects and object collection. Vector's are used to represent attributes of entities like: age, name, sex and scores etc. and this can be anything.
